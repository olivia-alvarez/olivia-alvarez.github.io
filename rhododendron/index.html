Pacific Rhododendron

Washington's State Flower: The Pacific Rhododendron

Name of Plant:

Pacific Rhododendron

Common Alternate Names of the Plant:

California Rhododendron, Coast Rhododendron, or California Rosebay.

Range of Physical Size:

Can reach heights anywhere from 3ft to 24ft tall, growing taller in shaded areas.

Range of Geography:

This plant is found throughout southern British Columbia to Northern California.

Climate:

Grows in arid and dry climates, and is commonly found in the rainshadow of the Olympic Mountains.
