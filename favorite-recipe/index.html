Pecan Pie

The best pecan pie recipe, a staple for the holiday season.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole unbaked pie crust
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 Tbsp. brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup light corn syrup
  • 1/3 cup melted salted butter
  • 3 whole eggs, beaten
  • 3/4 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 heaping c. chopped pecans
