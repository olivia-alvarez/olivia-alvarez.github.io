Watermelon Mint Mojito (Mocktail)

A refreshing watermelon mojito, suitable for all ages.

Yields: 1 servings

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 0 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cold, fresh watermelon cubes
  • 4 mint leaves
  • 1/2 ounce (1 Tbsp) of lime juice
  • plain sparkling water
  • ice cubes, enough to fill glass halfway
  • lime slice and mint leaves for garnish

Directions

  1. Prepare watermelon and cut accordingly. (A precut cup of watermelon from the store can be be substituted to make this step faster)
  2. Place cut watermelon and mint leaves in a tall glass, lightly muddle until blended together.
  3. Add in lime juice and ice.
  4. Top with sparkling water, garnish with a lime slice and mint leaves (if desired).
  5. Sit back, relax, and enjoy!
