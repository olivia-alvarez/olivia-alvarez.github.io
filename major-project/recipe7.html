Easy Mexican Rice

A classic side dish to pair with any Latin meal.

Yields: 6 servings

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp canola oil or vegetable oil
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups hot water
  • 1/2 cup of diced yellow onion
  • 2 teaspoons Caldo de Tomate (tomato bouillon)

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a 3-quart saute pan over medium heat. Add rice and stir to combine. Cook, stirring frequently, for 3-5 minutes, until rice begins to turn a golden color.
  2. Measure 2 cups of hot water in a liquid measuring cup, add Caldo de Tomate to hot water and stir until dissolved.
  3. Add hot water mixture to the rice in the saute pan, be very careful as water will boil and produce hot steam.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low and cover. Simmer for 20 minutes, or until all water has been absorbed.
  5. Turn off the heat and let the rice rest, add in diced onion, cover for 5-10 minutes. Fluff rice with a fork and serve.
