Mexican Street Corn (Elote)

Classic elote, extra love.

Yields: 4 servings

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Ingredients

For the corn:

  • 4 cobs sweet corn, peeled
  • 3 Tbsp butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup cotija cheese, grated
  • chopped cilantro, optional for garnishing
  • tajin spice, optional for garnishing

For the sauce:

  • 3/4 tsp tajin spice
  • 3 Tbsp mayo
  • 3 Tbsp Mexican style crema
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 2 Tbsp chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 whole lime, juiced, about 1 tablespoon

Directions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. Mix together the sauce ingredients and set it in the fridge until you are ready for it.
  3. Melt the butter and brush the corn cobs with it. Wrap in aluminum foil wrap and grill for about 15-18 minutes until cobs are blistered, flipping them around every 3-5 minutes or so.
  4. Remove from the grill and unwrap. Remove from the wrap and add to a plate. Let the cobs cool for a few minutes and then brush the sauce mixture on them. Top with cotija cheese, some more tajin sauce and chopped parsley.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy.
