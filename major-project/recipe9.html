Homemade Tortillas

Simple ingredients, generations of comfort.

Yields: 8-10 servings

Prep Time: 1 hour 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup warm water

Directions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and baking powder.
  2. Add the melted butter to the dry ingredients, mixing until well incorporated.
  3. Gradually add warm water, stirring until a dough forms.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest for at least 1 hour in a Ziploc bag or covered bowl.
  5. After resting, form the dough into small balls. Flatten each ball with a rolling pin to form tortillas, bigger tortillas will be thinner.
  6. Cook the tortillas on a hot shallow pan, turning once bubbles form for a light golden finish on both sides.
