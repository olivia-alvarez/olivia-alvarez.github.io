Mom's Picadillo

The recipe that always brought us to the table, made with love-always.

Yields: 6 servings

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup onion diced
  • 2 cups potato peeled and diced
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 1/2 cup beef broth, (Caldo de Res)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp ground cumin

Directions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat, add ground beef. Cook over medium heat while breaking up the meat. Cook until mostly browned, drain grease and return to heat.
  2. Add in diced onion and potatoes. Continue to cook over medium heat until onion is softened. Add garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds.
  3. Prepare beef broth, (1 Tbsp of Caldo de Res seasoning to 1 cup of water). Pour broth into the skillet.
  4. Add in seasonings: salt, pepper, and cumin. Stir to combine.
  5. Bring mixture to a simmer, cover and let cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Remove lid, stir, and continue cooking with the lid off for an additional 10 minutes or until potatoes are softened and most of the liquid is gone.
  7. Serve warm with flour tortillas and rice.
